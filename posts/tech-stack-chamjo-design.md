In August 2021 we decided to initiate and execute the ideas by starting with an MVP. In September 2021, we proudly launch our first MVP. The thing to know is that The Tech team made **Chamjo** by chasing airtime **for 2 weeks**, how do we **solve** it? That can happen because we have to choose the right technology stack.

## Tech Stack

Using the right technology stack will really help us reduce our workload. And of course, we think carefully about what technology we should use. In the case of Chamjo, the technology used for Frontend is we are using [**React Js**](https://reactjs.org/), and [**Tailwind**](https://tailwindcss.com) helps us to beautify CSS. And then we use [**Node Js**](https://nodejs.org), and [**Express js**](https://expressjs.com/) as Backend.
